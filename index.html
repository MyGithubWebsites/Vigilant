How Vigilant Works

  1. Report: Users submit suspicious job postings or recruiter behavior through our secure form.
  2. Verify: Our team and community checks evidence, company registration, and multiple data points.
  3. Publish: Verified warnings and safe company badges are shown to help others make informed choices.
  4. Support: We guide victims towards resources and encourage employers to resolve issues publicly.

Quick Tips

  • Never pay upfront for a job offer.
  • Check company domain emails — beware of public email addresses.
  • Verify company registration and LinkedIn profiles.
  • Ask for an official offer letter on company letterhead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I report anonymously?

Yes — the system accepts anonymous reports. We encourage non-identifying evidence (screenshots, links) to help verification.

What happens after I report?

Reports enter review. If a pattern emerges we publish a warning and flag the company as ‘Flagged’. We may contact the company for clarification.

How do you verify employers?

Verification involves cross-checking official registries, the company website, public reviews, and corroborating user reports.
